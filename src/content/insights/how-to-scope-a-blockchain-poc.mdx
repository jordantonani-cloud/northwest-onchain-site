## Outline

- Start from the outcome, not the technology: define the one claim to prove or kill.
- Turn the outcome into a metric (e.g. settlement time, cost per transaction, error rate).
- Map the minimal integration surface needed to test that metric honestly.
- Define the kill criteria: what result means "stop."
- Attach the team and the budget to the metric, not to hours.
