// Why now

For years the blocker wasn't the technology. It was the rules.

That's changing. A federal stablecoin law is on the books, and digital-asset market-structure rules are advancing. The institutions that build now will be ready when the rules fully land.

GENIUS ActSigned into law · 2025

The first U.S. federal framework for payment stablecoins — one-to-one reserves, permitted issuers. It turns stablecoin settlement from a gray area into a sanctioned rail. (Rulemaking is still rolling out.)

CLARITY ActAdvancing · not yet law

Market-structure legislation that would divide oversight and reduce the "is it a security?" risk stalling tokenization. It cleared the Senate Banking Committee in May 2026 and still needs a full Senate vote.

Regulatory status as of June 2026. Directional, not legal advice.

// Who we are & how we work

We're the bridge from boardroom to blockchain — the rare team fluent in both a financial model and a smart contract.

Northwest Onchain is a blockchain strategy and delivery practice, not a vendor. We don't sell a chain or a token. We help banks, investors, and operators decide whether — and how — to move onchain, then bring the right team to deliver it.

It's a considered process, not a transaction. We start by understanding the business, map the real opportunity (including when there isn't one), define the metrics and the cost, and assemble a vetted network of builders to deliver against them. Most engagements begin with a short, low-commitment audit — a way to see if there's something real here before anyone commits to a build.
